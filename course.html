Why Not Some Other Website?

Why This Website?

I have collected the best and ideal information from different textbooks and videos.

One Whole Curriculum

As a programmer, I understand and I have experienced the struggles of learning a new language. It's not easy! Even with that in mind, I explored many youtube videos and many websites; but even with that help, it was very tough learning the language. Every site had details of a small section, and not a full curriculum. I have made VetPy in such a way, that you won't need to search the internet for different sections!

Fun Reading!

I remember when researching for my project, I ran into many websites, where they had so much information, that the whole website was just info. There was no examples or anything, Just Information! Not a case with VetPy. I made this site also so users can enjoy tackling new topics while enjoying reading!
